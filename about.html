Saitama Genos

Saitama Genos, born 22 August 1970, is a Japanese MasterChef who worked and owned a restaurant in Tokyo for more than 20 years. His creativity with food and strive for perfection has earned him many awards and recognition in the culinary industry.
In 2018 he sold his restaurant in Tokyo and moved to London to be closer to his family. A year later he started a new business in London to continue to share his passion and love for food with his customers, this time by hosting private dining experience at his restaurant.

Dine with us

To ensure the maximum taste experience of each customer the restaurant has only has ten seats available. Customers will only be served by reservation, please use the contact form to make a reservation. Please note that a reservation can take up to three months.
The taste menu is chosen by Saitama and will change every month depending on the season and the availability of the ingredients on the market. If you have any allergies, please include this on the contactform and Saitama will ensure you will have the same taste experience.
