Saitama Genos

Saitama Genos, born 29 June 1975, is a Japanese chef and owner of Saitama Dainingu. He is born and raised in Tokyo, Japan. After high school he started to work at a well known high-class sushi restaurant in Tokyo. For more than 20 years he has worked with the best chefs in the world. His creativity with food and strive for perfection has earned him many awards and recognition in the culinary industry.
In 2018 he decided to move to London to be closer to his family. A year later he opened Saitama Dainingu to share his passion and love for food with his customers.
