A Private Dining Experience

With so many commercial Japanese restaurants over the world, it is hard to find a quiet place where you can enjoy the authentic taste of Japan. At Saitama Dainingu you can close your eyes, hear the sound of cooking, and smell the scent of the best ingredients being prepared for you by a highly skilled chef.

MasterChef Saitama is a professional Japanese chef who sold his restaurant in Tokyo and moved to London to be closer to his family. He wants to share his passion and love for food with his customers by hosting private dining experience at his restaurant. The concept of his restaurant is different his competitors. The restaurant only has ten seats available and will only serve dinner to his customers by reservation. The taste menu is chosen by Saitama and will change every week depending on the season and the availability of ingredients on the market. This ensures that every customer will get the full taste experience of Saitama’s cooking.
